%HThe hour (24-hour clock), padded to 2 digits (00-23)09
%IThe hour (12-hour clock), padded to 2 digits (01-12)09

%kThe hour (24-hour clock), padded with a leading space for single digit values (0-23) 9
%lThe hour (12-hour clock), padded with a leading space for single digit values (1-12) 9
%mThe month, padded to 2 digits (01-12)10
%nNewline character
%MThe minute, padded to 2 digits (00-59)50
%p"AM" or "PM"AM
%P"am" or "PM"am

%sN umber of seconds since the Epoch (1970-01-01 00:00:00 +0000), UTC1444204240

%tTab character

%UWeek number of the year, Sunday as the first day of the week, padded to 2 digits (00-53)40

%WWeek number of the year, Monday as the first day of the week, padded to 2 digits (00-53)40
%yThe year without the century (00-99)15
%YThe year with the century2015
