Strftime Helper

Code Meaning Example
%a Abbreviated weekday name Mon
%A Full weekday name Monday
%w The weekday, Sunday as the first day of the week (0-6) 1
%d Day of the month, padded to 2 digits (01-31) 28
%b Abbreviated month name Jul
%B Full month name July
%m The month, padded to 2 digits (01-12) 07
%y The year without the century (00-99) 14
%Y The year with the century 2014
%H The hour (24-hour clock), padded to 2 digits (00-23) 18
%I The hour (12-hour clock), padded to 2 digits (01-12) 06
%p "AM" or "PM" PM
%M The minute, padded to 2 digits (00-59) 01
%S The second, padded to 2 digits (00-60) 36
%z The time zone offset from UTC, with a leading plus sign for UTC and zones east of UTC and a minus sign for those west of UTC, hours and minutes follow each padded to 2 digits and with no delimiter between them -0400
%Z The time zone name, replaced with an empty string if it is not found EDT
%j Day of the year, padded to 3 digits (001-366) 209
%U Week number of the year, Sunday as the first day of the week, padded to 2 digits (00-53) 30
%W Week number of the year, Monday as the first day of the week, padded to 2 digits (00-53) 30
